About My Work

My experience & skills as a web developer....

I'm a Web Developer in the Dallas, TX area, who creates sites that are fast, user friendly, interactive, & responsive. My projects consist of custom HTML & PHP websites, as well as custom Wordpress themes built from scratch.

I've worked with common front-end frameworks like bootstrap & jQuery, and I'm quick and efficient with CSS & JavaScript. I've also worked with JavaScript APIs like the YouTube and Google Maps APIs. On the back-end, I've worked regularly with PHP to improve site organization and functionality in both cPanel and Wordpress.

Also, I'm familiar with technologies and have skills that supplement web development, such as: simple logo & graphic design via Photoshop; a good understanding of Google Search Console and best SEO practices; and knowledge of web traffic concepts via Google Analytics.
